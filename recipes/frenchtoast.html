French Toast

Description

Sliced bread soaked in eggs and cream, then pan fried and topped with powdered sugar and syrup.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • bread (any type)
  • cream
  • vanilla extract
  • butter
  • powdered sugar
  • cinnamon
  • syrup
  • lemon juice
  • salt

Steps

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  2. Lightly butter a griddle and heat over medium-high heat.
  3. Dunk bread in the egg mixture (eggs, cream, cinnamon, vanilla).
  4. Transfer the bread to the hot skilled and cook until golden brown on each side (about 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Repeat with more pieces of bread until you have the desired amount.
  6. Top the finished pieces with butter, powdered sugar, a splash of lemon juice, and syrup.
