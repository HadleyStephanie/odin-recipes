PB & J

Description

A simple, yet delicious sandwhich made with peanut butter and jelly.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter (crunchy or creamy)
  • Jelly (any flavor)
  • Bread (any kind)

Steps

  1. Place two slices of bread on a plate or cutting board.
  2. Using a knife, spread peanut butter on one slice of the bread.
  3. Using a spoon, scoop jelly and then spread it on the slice of bread that has nothing on it.
  4. Put the two slices of bread together (peanut butter side to jelly side).
  5. Cut sandwhich and enjoy!
